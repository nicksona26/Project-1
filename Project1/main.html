Here's a timeline of Josh's career

  • 1991 Kyuss released their debut album Wretch
  • 1992 Kyuss released their album Blues for the Red Sun
  • 1994 Kyuss released their album Welcome to Sky Valley
  • 1995 Kyuss released their album ...And the Circus Leaves Town
  • 1996-98 Josh was the second-guitar player for Screaming Trees
  • 1997 Josh co-produced Fu-Manchu's album Godzilla
  • 1997 Josh co-produced Beaver's album The Difference Engine
  • 1998 Queens of the Stone Age release their debut self-titled album
  • 1998 Desert Sessions release The Desert Sessions Volume 1 & 2
  • 1998 Desert Sessions release The Desert Sessions Volume 3 & 4
  • 1999 Desert Sessions release The Desert Sessions Volume 5 & 6
  • 2000 Queens of the Stone Age release their album Rated R
  • 2001 Desert Sessions release The Desert Sessions Volume 7 & 8
  • 2002 Queens of the Stone Age release their album Songs for the Deaf
  • 2003 Desert Sessions release The Desert Sessions Volume 9 & 10
  • 2004 Eagles of Death Metal released their album The Desert Sessions Volume 9 & 10
  • 2004 Eagles of Death Metal released their album Heart On
  • 2005 Queens of the Stone Age release their album Lullabies to Paralyze
  • 2006 Eagles of Death Metal released their album Death by Sexy
  • 2007 Queens of the Stone Age release their album Era Vulgaris
  • 2009Them Crooked Vultures release their self-titled debut album Them Crooked Vultures
  • 2009 Josh co-produced The Arctic Monkey's album Humbug
  • 2012 Josh co-produced The Hives's album Lex Hives
  • 2013 Queens of the Stone Age release their album ...Like Clockwork
  • 2015 Eagles of Death Metal released their album Zipper Down
  • 2016 Josh co-produced and played guitar on Iggy Pop's album Post-Pop Depression
  • 2017 Queens of the Stone Age release their album Villains
  • 2019 Eagles of Death Metal released their album Boots Electric Performing the Best Songs We Never...
  • 2019 Desert Sessions release The Desert Sessions Volume 11 & 12
  • 2021 Josh co-produced Royal Blood's album Typhoons
